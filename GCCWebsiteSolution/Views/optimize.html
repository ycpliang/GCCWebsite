Why Sponsor OPTIMIZE ?

OPTIMIZE is hosted by GCC Waterloo, a chapter of GCC Global boasted over 10,000 members within their network. As the largest chapter of GCC Global, GCC Waterloo has experienced an exceptional growth rate over the past year of 15%, with an overall growth rate of 67% since 2012.

Beyond the OPTIMIZE workshops, GCC Waterloo offers professional development events that are open for all students but has been especially popular with students in Accounting and Financial Management, Financial Analysis and Risk Management, and Mathematics/Chartered Professional Accounting programs.
