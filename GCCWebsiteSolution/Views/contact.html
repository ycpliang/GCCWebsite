Story

Global China Connection (GCC) is an international student organization dedicated to providing young professionals and future leaders with a platform to engage with one another. As a U.S. based non-profit organization, GCC is represented in over 60 universities across the globe. The GCC network connects students to top universities and corporate partners in multiple countries including Canada, the United States, and China. GCC Waterloo aims to create new opportunities for collaboration between the leaders of tomorrow through hosting conferences, case competitions, and networking opportunities. GCC Waterloo focuses on supporting students on their academic journey and career path by connecting YOU to the right people and resources. From a fourth-year Engineering student with entrepreneurship ambitions, to an incoming Arts student looking to discover their career path, we aim to make a meaningful impact in the growth and development of each and every student at the University of Waterloo.
